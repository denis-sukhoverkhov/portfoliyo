{# Features ------------------------------------- #}

Easy parent sign-up

Parents text a code they get from teachers to get started, and Portfoliyo takes care of the rest. Or: Teachers can sign parents up manually.

Two-way communication

Teachers can have one-on-one conversations with a parent or send updates to the entire class.

Multiple languages

Teachers can communicate with every student's family in their own language.

Work as a team

Invite other teachers and family members, to keep every child's village working together.

Simple, secure records

Keep safe & secure records of all parent communication, without the hassle of paperwork.
